Welcome To GSB

Gateway School of Business(GSB) is offering 3 years full time BBA programme and 2 years MBA programme and is affiliated to Deenbandhu Choturam University of Science and Technology.

GSB is based on the knowledge that the leaders of tomorrow must have a global mindset and an entrepreneurial spirit. This program is a full-time course of study that leads to a first step in management degree while helping you develop your vision and the skills to achieve it. The education you receive at GSB will empower you with the knowledge, skills, and long-term vision that lead to innovation and growth.

Key Features BBA & MBA

  • Effective Personality Development Program through PD studios
  • GST Training
  • Advanced Digital Marketing Program and Live Projects
  • Advanced training on MS Excel
  • A platform for direct communication b/w Students and Industrial Experts
